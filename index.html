Scott Nelson is an Intermedia Composer and Educator
whose current research is centered on exploring the suitability of
Autonomous Sensory Meridian Response (ASMR) within various art forms, seeking new possibilities in the realm of sensory aesthetics.
Scott aims to create immersive, multidimensional experiences
that invite participants to play an active role in the creation of the art.
