Scott Nelson is an Intermedia Composer and Educator whose current research is centered on investigating the suitability of immersive audio technology for eliciting Autonomous Sensory Meridian Response (ASMR). The immersive audio technologies being primarily investigated are wave-field synthesis, spatial audio in virtual reality, and mobile device networks. Scott aims to create immersive, multidimensional experiences that invite participants to play an active role in the creation of the art.
